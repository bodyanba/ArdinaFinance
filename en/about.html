About us
What is the journey you want to embark on?

Ardina's team understands where you want to go and will guide you on the route that we will navigate together.

Ardina Finance is an investment advisory company located in Panama, registered and regulated by the Superintendencia del mercado de valores of Panama under resolution SMV No.43-18 of February 1, 2018. Ardina Finance guides you as you navigate the financial markets. We offer comprehensive financial advice, regardless of the jurisdiction and financial institution you use to protect your assets. Our goal is to create a successful relationship of trust in the long term and become a leading boutique provider in the wealth management sector. We seek the best investment alternatives, focusing on innovative, personal and profitable solutions.

Panama

We are based in Panama, at the meeting point of the world’s two largest oceans and where two continents join. Panama is the financial hub of Latin America, thanks to its geographical situation, political stability and dollarized, competitive economy. Ardina Finance has its headquarters in Panama, but works with several jurisdictions depending on the client’s needs and depository banks.

Our Team

Carlos Alberto Modi
Founder and director at Ardina Finance

With over 20 years’ experience, Carlos Alberto Modi started his career with UBS and later worked for Credit Suisse and Deutsche Bank. In 2008, he joined the private bank LGT. His experience in asset management and risk analysis has allowed him to develop his own portfolio of private clients. As a holder of the CIIA (International Financial Analyst Diploma) and CIWM (International Diploma of Investment Advisor) qualifications, he also holds a Chief Executive Officer licence No. 563, according to SMV Resolution No. 353-16 of July 8, 2016 and a broker's licence No. 1391, according to resolution SMV No. 352-16 of July 8, 2016.

Cesar Zango Macias
Associate and director at Ardina Finance

Cesar Zango Macias has extensive experience in the financial and accounting sector, having worked for international groups such as L'Oréal, Terra Travel (Telefonica Group) and Andanza Technology. In 2013, he partnered with the 360 Consulting Group in Panama, advising SMEs and individuals on tax and financial matters.

Elka Lorena Segrera Castro
Associate at Ardina Finance

Angie Paola Segrera Castro
